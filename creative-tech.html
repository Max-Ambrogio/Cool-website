DDR - Project

When the nearest Dance Dance Revolution machine is in an arcade miles away, it's time for your fingers to come out and play. It's time for Dance Dance Revolution to get a 21st century, Gen Z makeover.

Using Blender, Tinkercad, C++ and an Arduino UNO, I put a modern twist on this classic game. Now, you can literally have Dance Dance Revolution in the palm of your hand. No need to travel far when your fingers start itching for a little bit of rhythm and a whole lot of dance.

F.P.V - Drone

In the recent years, the FPV (First Person View) drone has blossomed into popularity with it's cutting edge visuals, unique perspective and quick response times. Compared to traditional cinema drones, the FPV drone is innovative, sleek and unique in its footage.

With this project, each of the motors were soldered to the flight controller which. After doing this, the proper 12AWG power adapter was connected to the F.C. in order for it to be compatible with the correct LiPo batteries. All of these adjustments turned a regular group of materials into a fully functional FPV drone.
